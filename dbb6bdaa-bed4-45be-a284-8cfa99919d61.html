Description

Platform and fight past spikes and enemies in Jaded, a linear 2D side-scrolling platformer. Slow down time using your 360-degree directional dash, and lock on to targets to strike! See the snowy mountains, dense forests, volcanic caves, and abandoned laboratory. Killed by enemies or spikes? Instantly respawn at the start of the room, giving you an infinite number of tries to overcome the challenge!

The Jaded beta includes Level 1, Level 2, and half of Level 3, each taking at most an hour to beat. In its current state, players can expect finished levels, music, three save files, and secret collectibles scattered across the map to discover.
