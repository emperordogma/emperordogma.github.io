🔹🔷 FEATURES 🔷🔹

🎲 Over 60 challenging levels.
🎲 4 unique zones.
🎲 Dualplay - play with or without VR headset.
🎲 Compact, comfortable Play Area.
🎲 Truly brain-teasing puzzles and riddles.
🎲 Cube-based labyrinth with traps, quantum physics and out of box solutions.


🔹🔷 SUMMARY 🔷🔹

🎲 Neverout is a game like no other. It takes huge inspiration from the most classic 'Escape Room' in history. The game features reality bending, quantum
physics, traps, obstacles and puzzles. All soaked in otherworldly and surreal setting.

🎲 The players will test their imagination, sense of logic, 'out-of-the-box' thinking and how quick and swift they can adapt to ever-changing reality.

🎲 'Up' can become 'down', 'floor' - 'ceiling'. Fire might freeze and ice start burning. But there's always logic behind the madness and one thing is certain - there is always a solution.

But some...
Come with sacrifices.
