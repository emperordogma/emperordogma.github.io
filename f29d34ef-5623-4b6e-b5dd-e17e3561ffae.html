Description

Black Desert is an open-world MMORPG offering intense, action-packed gameplay alongside a wealth of engaging content—all played by over 20 million Adventurers worldwide. With its constant stream of updates, Black Desert has been providing players with an endless number of fresh and exciting adventures. Take up the offer to step into a world full of unforgettable moments.

KEY FEATURES:



A WIDE, VIVID WORLD


The world of Black Desert consists of many vast territories. Experience unrivaled visuals and sound effects as you explore the vibrant sceneries and unique stories of each territory.

  • A seamless open world with no loading time to enjoy and immerse yourself in.
  • High-quality graphics that are realistic and unique
  • Changes in day and night, weather, as well as different seasons create a vivid, dynamic world.
  • Classy sound tracks that perfectly captures the mood of each region.


UNIQUE CHARACTER CUSTOMIZATION


Customize your character down to the finest detail and create the protagonist you want to play in the world of Black Desert. Create and freely customize the character of your dreams.

  • Adjust the details of your character’s face, hair style, body type, and height with sliders and color palettes.
  • Change the outfit material and color through dyes.


INTENSE ACTION


Play with over 20 classes that each have their own defined combat styles. Enjoy the dynamic combat full of unique actions and commands that is exclusive to Black Desert.

  • Action-packed combat free of tab-targeting
  • In-depth action created from a variety of skills and seamless transition between skills
  • PvE and PvP that can be enjoyed in a variety of ways including killing, raids, and field combat
  • A large-scale war to claim the territories you adventure through.


ENDLESS NEW ADVENTURES


Experience a variety of activities including developing life skills, exploring the ocean, playing instruments, decorating a home, and more. It’s up to the Adventurers to decide which activity to enjoy and to what extent.

  • Life-related content including gathering, fishing, hunting, cooking, alchemy, processing, training, trading, farming, sailing, and bartering.
  • Mastery that can be accumulated through each skill and the close connection between the many different skills.


STORIES CREATED TOGETHER


There is a plethora of content where you can choose to compete or cooperate with other people online. Create precious memories by joining a guild or making new friends.

  • Content you can enjoy in guilds includes guild raids, guild missions, and more.
  • Co-op with friends by defeating monsters in parties and in dungeons.
