Description



Welcome to Slaughter League, the next global sports phenomenon. Pit yourself against seven others in a variety of intense multiplayer battles where the other competitors are not your only concern. Avoid incinerating flamethrowers, electrifying tesla coils, and of course, those good old-fashioned mutilating buzzsaws using a blend of decisive parkour platforming, all the while being hilariously narrated by our announcers.

Do you have what it takes to brave the Slaughter League and come out in one piece? Warning: the odds are not in your favour (hence the name)!


Squad up with up to three friends or play solo in Against All Odds’ stacked multiplayer offering. Whether you want to roast the rest of the world or get silly in custom games with friends or randoms, we’ve got you covered. With over 20+ official stages in matchmaking, a global custom games browser, and a forever growing user-generated stage browser - a new challenge in Slaughter League is never far away.

Participate and overcome classic set pieces in the Race mode where everybody shares the same goal: Survive the onslaught and reach the end intact. First over the line wins!

Hunt down other adversaries in Zombies, where infected competitors must outrun and contact the rest. Either survive the longest or infect as many healthy foes as possible.

Forget about crypto and NFTs, make bank in the ‘collect-a-thon’ mode Gold Rush where you must collect as many coins as humanly possible in the time limit.

We understand that your life is precious, and worth a lot to you, so you will be handsomely rewarded for your troubles with mountains of cash, fans, and experience. We also know its important to look the part when you’re slaying your foes. Unlock hundreds of unique cosmetic items from simple shorts and a vest to mystical wizard outfits which can all be freely mixed and matched to your liking.


There’s a lot of fun to be had watching your rivals be decimated, but there’s a lot more fun to be had when you’re the reason for that! Against All Odds features a robust stage editor allowing players to design the death-trap filled obstacle course of their dreams (or nightmares!)

Novice builders can use pre-made mode templates to build whatever crazy stage they desire with ease. For those looking to dig deep, Against All Odds also features a plethora of mode editing tools including an in-game script editor allowing unimaginable flexibility. The stage editor contains over 40+ unique objects to work with, each with their own customization options from color, supports, parenting and much more!

Share your creations with the world by publishing stages to the global stage browser or test your life-defying experimental concepts privately with friends. Creators are compensated for their hard work with unique rewards to use within the multiplayer for publishing popular stages.

There’s never been a better reason to sign your life away… Join Slaughter League today and defy all the odds!
